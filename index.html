About Me

Olga Pearce is a computer scientist in the Center for Applied Scientific Computing (CASC) at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL). Her research interests include distributed data structures and parallel algorithms; parallel programing models; performance analysis, optimization, and modeling; and application load balancing. Olga joined LLNL in 2009 as a Lawrence Scholar working with Bronis de Supinski, and joined CASC in 2014. Olga received her Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2014 from Texas A&M University.
